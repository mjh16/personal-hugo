Hummus: Garlic and Roasted Red Pepper

This week I decided to make hummus to take to the office to share as a snack and meal accessory. I gathered my supplies and set out to make the two batches: one roasted red pepper and the other garlic.From there it was a quick assembly in my Blendtec Total Blender, blend it, package it, and take it to school with some tortilla chips and some carrots. It’s been good so far and a great, easy, and healthy snack to bring. It is cheaper and almost as easy as buying the hummus from the store.
